# Workbench v3.3.0 — Platform Handoffs and Shared Evidence

## Scope

- Shared evidence records with stable identifiers, citations, uncertainty, freshness, licensing, artifacts, provenance, and SHA-256 hashes.
- Site Intelligence → Workbench evidence and dataset packets.
- Decision Studio → Workbench modeling requests.
- Workbench → Decision Studio calculation, scenario, sensitivity, and validation packets.
- Research Librarian → Workbench guided research routes.
- Workbench → Research Librarian evidence-gap and citation packets.
- Cross-application project links and target receipts.
- Compatibility reports for required capabilities and offline delivery.
- Portable JSON handoff bundles when live integrations are unavailable.
- Private authenticated WordPress records with browser-local operation for public visitors.

## Boundaries

Handoffs do not automatically approve, publish, certify, execute, or accept decisions. Receiving applications and human reviewers must verify packet hashes, evidence provenance, uncertainty, citations, and compatibility before creating derivative records.
