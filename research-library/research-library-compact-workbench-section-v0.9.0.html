Analytical Workbench

Ask the Library, Run the Calculators, Analyze the Equations

The Sustainable Catalyst Workbench adds an article-aware analytical layer to the Research Library. It can ask site-scoped questions, run backend calculators, generate graphs and reports, detect LaTeX equations from WordPress content, map equations to relevant tools, and connect results back to article maps, methods, code, and interpretation.
