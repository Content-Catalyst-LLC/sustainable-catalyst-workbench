# Workbench v5.0.0 — Sustainable Catalyst Integrated Research and Engineering Platform

Version 5.0.0 unifies the mature Workbench layers into one canonical project and controlled-release system. It connects guided project creation, persistent and team workspaces, data pipelines, scientific and engineering analysis, Lab workflows, Site Intelligence, Decision Studio, Research Librarian, Knowledge Library, shared evidence, review, evaluation, extensions, WordPress persistence, and offline installations.

The release adds an Integrated Platform studio, canonical platform projects and portfolios, cross-surface workflows, surface registries, integrity audits, governance gates, deployment plans, technical dossiers, and portable content-addressed packages.

All execution, publication, certification, device control, destructive synchronization, and high-stakes decisions remain explicitly human controlled.
