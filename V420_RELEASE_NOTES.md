# Workbench v4.2.0 — Workflow Templates and Guided Scientific/Engineering Project Creation

## Purpose

v4.2.0 turns the connected Workbench into a guided project-start environment. It provides reusable templates, structured intake, requirement and milestone plans, validation gates, starter-evidence placeholders, team bindings, and portable project scaffolds.

## Built-in workflows

- Scientific Investigation
- Engineering Design and Verification
- Instrument, Sensor, and Measurement Validation
- Predictive Analytics and Model Evaluation
- Sustainability Systems Assessment
- Laboratory Evidence to Decision Brief

## Boundaries

Templates do not execute experiments, operate devices, approve claims, publish records, certify compliance, or replace qualified review. All starter evidence is marked unverified until a human attaches and reviews real sources.
