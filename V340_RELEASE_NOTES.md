# Sustainable Catalyst Workbench v3.4.0

## Collaboration, Review, and Technical Sign-Off

- Reviewer roles and private review queues
- Comments attached to exact project records and field paths
- Requested changes, assignment, resolution, and accepted-risk records
- Requirements-to-review traceability
- Canonical revision comparison
- Immutable content-hashed review snapshots
- Internal and qualified-professional sign-off scopes
- Credential references for qualified review
- Exportable review and approval dossiers
- Explicit boundary between internal approval and licensed professional certification

The release preserves every v2.x and v3.x studio and adds Collaboration & Sign-Off as the sixteenth unified Workbench studio.
