# Workbench v3.6.0 Responsible-Use Boundary

- No arbitrary remote model training is enabled by this release.
- No autonomous high-stakes decision execution is exposed.
- Leakage, drift, fairness, and uncertainty outputs are diagnostic evidence, not legal or regulatory certification.
- Medical, employment, credit, education, insurance, benefits, safety, and legal uses require qualified domain review and independent validation.
- Protected attributes are excluded from ordinary feature plans by default and retained only for justified auditing.
- Model cards preserve intended uses, prohibited uses, limitations, reviewer identity, and approval state.
