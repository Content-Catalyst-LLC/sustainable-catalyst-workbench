# Workbench v3.7.0 Domain Laboratory Boundary

- Workbench builds plans and records; it does not independently execute physical experiments.
- Biological, biomedical, health, aerospace, propulsion, civil, and other safety-relevant work requires qualified review.
- Biomedical and health modules are non-diagnostic and do not replace clinical judgment.
- Civil, architectural, electrical, mechanical, aerospace, and similar outputs are not licensed professional certification.
- Synchronization never performs destructive overwrites without backup and explicit conflict resolution.
- Domain validation contracts preserve missing checks and never auto-certify an artifact.
- Notebook and report outputs remain drafts until human review.
