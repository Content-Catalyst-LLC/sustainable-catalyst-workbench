# Sustainable Catalyst Workbench v0.8.0 — Professional Systems Layer

This release adds a professional systems layer for advanced public-interest analysis.

## Major modules

- Predictive analytics: forecasting, time-series diagnostics, uncertainty proxies, model evaluation, reporting.
- Economics: macro scenarios, demand forecasting, fiscal multipliers, cost-benefit streams, econometrics and policy evaluation.
- FPGA/digital systems: timing slack, Fmax, fixed-point range, pipeline throughput, resource pressure.
- Electrical/power/RF: three-phase power, voltage drop, power-factor correction, fault-current proxy, RF/antenna tools from v0.7.3.
- Mechanical/structural/civil: torsion, vibration, fatigue proxies, beam deflection, buckling, load combinations, stormwater, water demand, infrastructure risk.
- Urban planning and architecture: density, land-use mix, affordability, HVAC load proxy, daylight proxy, egress/occupancy, embodied carbon.
- Astrophysics: blackbody spectrum, luminosity, low-redshift Hubble estimates, Kepler periods.

## Professional guardrails

The Workbench supports education, exploratory modeling, research organization, and decision support. It does not replace licensed engineering, medical, legal, planning, code-compliance, clinical, safety, or laboratory judgment.
